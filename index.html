    Master Plan

    Burj Azizi's master plan by Azizi Developments intricately weaves together luxury residences, a seven-star hotel, and a vertical mall. The design harmonizes aesthetics with functionality, promising a seamless integration of living, leisure, and commerce in one iconic structure. The tower is envisioned as a self-sustaining urban environment where residents and visitors can live, work, and play in a single, cohesive space. The design focuses on maximizing the use of space while maintaining a sense of openness and connectivity.

    The 7-star hotel is another key element of the development, offering an exclusive hospitality experience that caters to the most discerning guests. The hotel will feature a range of luxurious suites, fine dining restaurants, and world-class amenities, making it a destination in itself. The vertical mall, spread over several floors, is designed to provide a unique shopping experience that combines retail with stunning views of the city.
